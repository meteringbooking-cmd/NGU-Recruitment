Do you know a fully qualified Smart Meter Engineer who would be a great addition to NGU?

Refer them to us and earn £1,000 for every successful candidate who joins the business.

How the Bonus is Paid

The £1,000 referral bonus is paid in instalments over a 12-month period.

Example: Engineer start date: 03/03/2025

· 1st Payment: June 2025
· 2nd Payment: September 2025
· 3rd Payment: December 2025
· Final Payment: March 2026

Important Information

  • Referrals will not be valid if the candidate has previously applied or has been referred by another employee within the last 6 months.
  • If the referred engineer submits their notice during the payment period, any remaining bonus payments will be cancelled.
  • Candidates who have worked for NGU within the last 6 months are not eligible.
  • Referrals must be submitted before interview and before completion of Team HRS.
  • The referral form must be completed in full and as accurately as possible.

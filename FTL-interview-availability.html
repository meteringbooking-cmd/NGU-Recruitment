Section 1: Background & Career History
  • Tell us about your background and recent roles.
  • What smart metering contracts or utility providers have you worked on?
  • Why are you looking to move roles?
  • What interests you about joining NGU?
Section 2: Qualifications & Technical Competence
  • What qualifications, registrations, or accreditations do you hold?
  • Are all certifications current and in date?
  • Do you hold any additional training (EV, Solar, Utilities etc.)?
  • What does a productive and compliant working day look like to you?
  • How many exchanges/jobs would you expect to complete while maintaining quality?
Section 3: Safety & Problem Solving
  • How do you balance productivity, customer service, and compliance?
  • Describe a time you solved a technical issue independently.
  • What would you do if you identified a safety risk at a property?
  • How would you manage a job overrunning with a frustrated customer?
Section 4: Customer Service & Behaviour
  • Tell us about a difficult customer situation and how you handled it.
  • How do you ensure each customer receives a positive experience?
  • How do you work under pressure or changing schedules?
Section 5: Reliability & Availability
  • This role requires flexibility. How do you manage changing workloads?
  • We operate a 1 in 6 callout rota. Any issues with this?
  • Is there anything affecting attendance, travel, or scheduling?
  • Do you have any pre-booked holidays in the next 6 months?
  • How soon could you start?
